History of Our Club

History of ISN Football Club

Based in Saint-Trivier-sur-Moignans, « Informatique et Sciences du Numérique Football Club” abbreviated in ISN Football Club, is a familial french football club builded in 2015. The club has at that time only one senior male team. In its first season in 2016-2017, the team finished second in the D6 and was eliminated from "Group Cup" in the third round, a great performance for a club that has just been created. Continuing on its good momentum, the club created the following season a second senior men's team. The first team in D5 ranks 6th at the end of the season and achieving the feat of reaching the final of the "Group Cup" but was severely corrected 3-0 against the arch-enemy Dombes Bresse Football Club. The second team ranks 10th of D6. The sports complex, located in Saint-Trivier-sur-Moignans, was the subject of many renovations during this season, the locker rooms of the team were redone to new and a synthetic field is installed. Cost of the project : +250 000€.

Last Result!

Heavy defeat in the final of "Group Cup"

Dombes Bresse Football Club 3 - 0 ISN Football Club
Final of the “Group Cup”Chatillon-sur-Chalaronne
Samedi 24 Mars 2018, 20h00

Summary of the match:
The ISN Football Club team achieved the feat of qualifying for the Claus Cup final by defeating Thoissey Football Club 2-1. The team aligned by coach Lucas Hervé is unsurprising:




























The bench is composed of: Loris De Araujo, the young Brazilian talent and Jimmy De Sa, aka “The Baron Rouge”.


The match begins at 8 pm on the grass of the communal stadium of Chatillon-sur-Chalaronne. ISN FC players get some chances early in the game, but can’t convert them. Louis Muller missed a big face-to-face in the 19th minute of play and Maxime Badot a head on corner in the 36th minute of play. The team was less and less vigilant at the end of the first period and concedes a goal just before half-time on a mistake of catch ball by Benjamin Cinquin. The team, enlisted by coach Lucas Hervé at half-time, returns to the field galvanized and determined to win the match. The injury of captain Jordan Chapuis in the 75th minute of play replaced by Jimmy De Sa aka "The baron rouge" is a blow. The team conceded a goal immediately on a free kick conceded by Jimmy De Sa a few seconds after entering the field. He receives a yellow card. The end of the match is sad for our players, Jimmy De Sa scooped a second yellow card in the 81st minute of play for an attack from behind on the No. 10 of Dombes Bresse Football Club. He is logically excluded from the field. The team then takes a third goal, and sees its hopes of winning the “Group Cup” fly away for this season.

Interview

Interview of the "Captain"

Just after the defeat in the final of "Group Cup", captain Jordan Chapuis sad and out on injury expresses himself at the microphone of Nanard:

- Nanard: Good evening Captain, bad evening for ISN Football Club, what do you think of this match?
- Jordan Chapuis: I think we had good opportunities in the first half with Louis and Maxime,
a pity that we have not managed to convert these good chances.
- Nanard: You came out on injury, how are you feeling?
- Jordan Chapuis: I have a pain in the thigh, I preferred to not force it, I imagine it is a
tear or a slight injury.
- Nanard: Will you be back soon then?
- Jordan Chapuis: Count on me very soon! We will have a drink and celebrate this beautiful
journey despite everything!
- Nanard: No injury is too severe for a beer! Good evening Captain!
